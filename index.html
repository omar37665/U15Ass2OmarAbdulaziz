Welcome to Our Charity Website!

This charity is a dedicated team of volunteers committed to uniting and empowering the Slovak community in South Manchester and its surrounding areas. We intend on providing education to children and adults who wish to join the Slovak community and actively keep cultural traditions alive through various workshops and cultural events.

Contact us for additional support at slovakaidfoundation@gmail.com
