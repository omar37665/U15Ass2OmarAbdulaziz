This picture perfectly captures the heart of what our Slovakian aid foundation is all about—bringing people together, sharing knowledge, and celebrating the things that matter. In this moment, a teacher is guiding a young girl through a reading activity as part of our special event for International Women’s Day (MDŽ). The day was all about teaching kids why we celebrate MDŽ and helping them express their appreciation for the amazing women in their lives. It’s moments like these—where kids feel supported, engaged, and inspired—that remind us why we do what we do.
